# Code of Conduct

AI Police is committed to respectful, accountable, and public-interest collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on evidence, reasoning, and safety impact.
- Welcome lawful, constructive disagreement.
- Protect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate, or discrimination.
- Advocacy for unlawful surveillance or extra-legal enforcement.
- Deliberate attempts to bypass safety and accountability controls.
- Misrepresentation of project capabilities.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the security contact process in SECURITY.md until a dedicated channel is established.
