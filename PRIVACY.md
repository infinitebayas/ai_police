# Privacy

AI Police is designed for lawful, accountable governance workflows.

Privacy compliance requirements vary by jurisdiction. Deploying entities are responsible for legal review and local compliance.

## Data Categories (Planned)

- Event metadata (space/session identifiers, timestamps).
- Moderation context (retrieval and policy references).
- Escalation and action logs.
- Optional transcript segments, if legally permitted.

## Privacy Commitments

- Data minimization by default.
- Purpose limitation and retention controls.
- Access controls and audit logging.
- Jurisdiction-specific deletion and appeal pathways.

## Deployment Responsibilities

Operators should define before deployment:

- legal basis for data processing,
- retention and deletion schedules,
- consent/notice requirements,
- cross-border transfer constraints,
- lawful access and review controls.

## Public Repo Limitation

This repository does not provide legal advice. It provides technical and governance scaffolding for lawful adaptation.
