# Jurisdiction Adaptation Matrix

Use this matrix to adapt AI Police for each country or region.

## Matrix Fields

- Jurisdiction name
- Legal basis for deployment
- Authorized operator types
- Protected speech and expression boundaries
- Cyber offense and public safety categories
- Escalation authority levels
- Human approval requirements
- Evidence and audit standards
- Retention and deletion rules
- Appeal and correction pathways
- Cross-border data constraints
- Public nodal support permissions and limits

- Require legal sign-off before operational rollout.
- Revalidate matrix when laws or policies change.
