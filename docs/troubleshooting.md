# Troubleshooting

## Problem: Unclear legal boundary for deployment

Action:

- Pause technical rollout.
- Complete jurisdiction profile in jurisdiction-adaptation-matrix.md.
- Obtain legal and policy sign-off.

## Problem: Escalation logic appears too aggressive

Action:

- Lower threshold settings.
- Add mandatory human review for medium/high-severity tiers.
- Validate policy pack assumptions.

## Problem: Public nodal support role is overreaching

Action:

- Restrict permissions to guidance and escalation only.
- Remove direct punitive action permissions.
- Add audit review for nodal actions.

## Problem: Conflicting policy requirements between regions

Action:

- Split jurisdiction packs.
- Assign separate deployment profiles.
- Avoid one global policy baseline for all legal contexts.
