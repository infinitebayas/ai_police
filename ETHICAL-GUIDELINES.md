# Ethical Guidelines

## Intended Use

AI Police is intended to support lawful, accountable public-interest moderation and cyber governance workflows.

## Prohibited Use

- Extra-legal targeting of individuals or groups.
- Political suppression through hidden rule manipulation.
- Collective punishment without review and accountability.
- Use that conflicts with constitutional, statutory, or human rights protections.

## Ethical Operating Standards

- Legality: operate within applicable law.
- Necessity: apply minimum required intervention.
- Proportionality: align response severity with harm.
- Accountability: preserve review, appeal, and remediation pathways.
- Transparency: disclose scope, limits, and governance model.

## Community and Public Nodal Support

Public-support participants should operate under documented governance, training standards, escalation rules, and oversight.

## Institutional Responsibility

Deploying institutions are accountable for lawful adaptation, outcomes, and corrective actions.
