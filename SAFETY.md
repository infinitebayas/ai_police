# Safety

AI Police aims to improve public-interest moderation governance while reducing harm.

## Safety Principles

- Human oversight for consequential actions.
- Proportionate response over punitive automation.
- Explainable guidance and decision traceability.
- Continuous monitoring for false positives and bias.

## Known Risks

- Context errors in language interpretation.
- Bias in policy datasets or lexical lists.
- Over-enforcement in sensitive or ambiguous speech contexts.
- Misuse of authority language by unauthorized operators.

## Safety Controls (Required in Deployment)

- Multi-level escalation with human approval gates.
- Audit logs with tamper-resistance.
- Appeal and correction workflows.
- Periodic policy review with local legal experts.

## Public Interest Guardrail

AI Police should be used to support lawful governance, not to suppress lawful speech or bypass due process.
